The SmartHotel Story

A vision born
from passion

SmartHotel was founded with a singular vision: to revolutionize the hospitality industry by seamlessly weaving cutting-edge technology into the fabric of world-class service. Since our inception, we have been committed to crafting guest experiences that are as memorable as they are innovative.

Our properties feature intelligent room controls, AI-powered concierge services, and sustainable practices that honor the environments we inhabit. Every detail is meticulously considered — because extraordinary experiences are built from extraordinary details.

The Founder

Meet the owner

Mehdi GHINE

Founder & Owner

"I built SmartHotel because I believe the future of luxury is not about opulence alone — it is about moments that feel genuinely personal."

With a passion for both technology and the art of hospitality, Mehdi GHINE envisioned a hotel brand that would stand apart — one where every guest is seen, every need is anticipated, and every stay becomes a memory. Under his leadership, SmartHotel has grown from a single riad in Marrakech to a globally recognized name in luxury hospitality.
